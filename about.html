Hi, my name is Jakub!

I'm 24-year-old student from Brno, CZ. I study Computer Science at Brno University of Technology. Lately I am interested in virtualized environments, statistics and machine learning. Last one I use in my master's thesis.

Since the start of my studies, I learnt a lot about different areas, even though if I had a choice, I wouldn't pick some of them. Also I travelled around Europe, which was the best opportunity any university can offer.

I was an intern in Red Hat for a year, in two teams, 6 months each. Mostly we were doing QE work on deployment tools and subscriptions, and a lot of virtualization. I was also selected for the internship at CERN - European Organization for Nuclear Research - as they have quite big IT department, mostly focused on big data processing and virtualization. I was working with their OpenStack Cinder backends.

I like to work on unique products, which will impact a lot of people and improve their lives. I love to collaborate with people who firmly believe in what they do and I want to help them, support them and work on the idea together. I find joy in helping great ideas to emerge, in shaping them and it doesn't matter, if I created them, or somebody else. I just want to be part of it and do my best.

I like Murakami's books, drinking tea and strategic, math heavy hobbies like investing and board games. I also started to do fencing.
